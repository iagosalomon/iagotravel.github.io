Travel Log

  • Gruta da agua viva

    Rio de janeiro, Brazil

    Hidden deep within a canyon, there is a secluded cave that can only be reached by boat. This hard to reach spot serves as a breeding ground for jellyfish, creating a purple color in the base of the rock.

  • Praia da Noivar

    Ilha Bela, Brazil

    Getting to this hidden beach in Brazil is an adventure on its own. After an hour-long hike along the cliffs, you arrive at an untouched stretch of sand with nothing but the ocean in front of you. There are no businesses or crowds—just powerful waves and surfers chasing the perfect wave."

  • Chacara do sol

    Cambui, brazil

    Hidden deep in the Brazilian countryside, this farm holds a unique surprise—a replica of a pond belonging to a famous member of the British royal family. Surrounded by nature and far from the busy world, it’s a quiet retreat where history and rural charm come together in an unexpected way.
